Welcome to EmpowerU online!

Doesn't it feel great to move! Whether it’s stretching out in yoga or shooting hoops in the gym, exercising just makes you feel awesome. However, you need to exercise your brain too - that's just as important!

Every week you will get to choose an activity to try from four categories: Physical, Nutritional, Mental and Relational.

Earn rewards for exercising and eating the right foods! Check out the prizes you can win just for taking care of yourself!

Rewards

Each week we will give you something new to try - but you must complete the Physical and Nutritional activity each week because you can't go back to earn past points. So don't skip your exercises or healthy snacks - we want you to get as many points as possible and win amazing prizes for participating!


Physical

Physical activity can lead to all kinds of benefits from better overall health to higher self-esteem to increased leadership skills.

Nutritional

You have the right to feel good and be healthy. Choose food that fuels your body and boosts brain power!

Mental

Learn to love who you are and find ways to stay calm in tough situations, crush limiting beliefs and become the person you were meant to be!

Relational

Learn how to embrace differences in others, develop amazing friendships and work through difficult feelings.
